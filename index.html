About Me

Greetings! Welcome to the How2AI4U Portfolio, where I share my passion for artificial intelligence and creative technology. I’m excited about exploring how AI can help us build amazing things, from coding smarter to creating useful everyday projects. Through the How2AI4U YouTube channel, I create videos that guide you step-by-step in using AI tools to bring your ideas to life. Whether you're interested in building websites, automating tasks, or generating creative content with AI, this is a space for you to learn and grow. This portfolio showcases some example projects that demonstrate what you can achieve using AI-powered tools. It’s built to inspire, to teach, and to help you discover how easy it can be to start making your own AI-driven solutions. I love learning new things and sharing practical knowledge with others. My goal is to make AI accessible, simple, and useful for everyone who wants to explore the future of technology. Curious about what you can create? Click the links below to explore.
